# Governance — مبادرة السيرة الرقمية

## Project Values

1. **Authenticity** — only verified Islamic content is published
2. **Openness** — anyone can contribute, all decisions are transparent
3. **Community** — this belongs to the Ummah, not any individual
4. **Quality** — scholar review is mandatory, never optional

---

## Decision Making

| Decision | Who decides |
|----------|-------------|
| Content accuracy | Scholar |
| Technical architecture | Trusted Committers |
| Roadmap priorities | Product Owner + TC consensus |
| New TC appointment | Existing TCs vote |
| Breaking changes | All TCs must approve |

---

## Becoming a Trusted Committer

A contributor becomes a Trusted Committer by:

1. Making at least 10 quality contributions
2. Being nominated by an existing TC
3. Approved by majority of existing TCs

---

## Removing a Trusted Committer

A TC may be removed by majority TC vote if they:

- Are inactive for 6+ months
- Violate the Code of Conduct
- Publish unverified Islamic content

---

## Scholar Review Panel

All Islamic content must be approved by the Scholar before publishing.
The Scholar role in Drupal has the final word on content accuracy.
